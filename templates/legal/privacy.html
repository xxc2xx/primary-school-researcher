Privacy

Last updated: 2026-05-23.

What we collect

  • Tool form inputs (citizenship, gender, target year, postal code, phase priorities): processed in-memory to compute your odds. Not stored unless you opt in to email capture.
  • Email address (only if you opt in): stored to send your report PDF and (max-monthly) updates. One-click unsubscribe in every email.
  • Anonymous analytics via Plausible: page URL, referrer, country. No cookies. No fingerprinting.
  • Payment data (only if you purchase): handled by Stripe. We see your email and Stripe customer ID — never your card details.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other third-party trackers.
  • We don't share data with schools or MOE.

Your rights (PDPA — Singapore)

  • Request a copy of your data: email privacy@lalac.sg.
  • Delete your data: email same address.
  • Withdraw email consent: unsubscribe link in any email.

Retention

  • Form inputs: not retained after the response is generated.
  • Email signups: retained until you unsubscribe + 30 days for backup hygiene.
  • Purchase records: retained 5 years for accounting (SG IRAS).

Contact

Data-protection officer: Lala C, privacy@lalac.sg.
