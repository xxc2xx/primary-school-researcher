About {{ site_name }}

I'm Lala C — an independent analyst. I built this because, when I started looking at Singapore P1 ballot data for my own family, I couldn't find one place that gave straight answers. MOE publishes the figures. Schlah ranks the schools. KiasuParents has the arguments. Nobody connects them to your specific case.

So I built that — first for myself, now for anyone who wants it. The model walks every MOE admission phase that applies to your family, ranks schools by realistic odds, and projects where each school's subscription is heading over the next 2-3 years.

What this is, and isn't

  • This is: data-driven analysis based on public MOE statistics. Personalised to your profile. Independent.
  • This isn't: affiliated with MOE or any school. Not a guarantee. Not a substitute for visiting schools or talking to other parents.

How I make money

The basic odds calculator is free, forever. If you want a full PDF report or a 1:1 strategy session, those are paid. That's it — no ads, no tracking, no selling your data.

Why I'm anonymous-ish

Lala C is a pen name. I keep my professional identity separate from this project so I can speak plainly without worrying about my day job. Everything you read here is my own view, on my own time, with my own money.
