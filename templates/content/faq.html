FAQ

Is Lala C affiliated with MOE?

No. We are independent. All ballot figures come from MOE public statistics, and we cite the source on every figure. We are not affiliated with any school.

Are the odds guaranteed?

No. The odds are estimates based on historical data and a projection model. MOE alone determines actual outcomes. Use the tool as one input among many.

Why is the basic tool free?

Because every Singapore family deserves clear information about how P1 admission actually works. Paid tiers cover the more bespoke work — printable reports and 1:1 strategy sessions.

Do you store my postal code?

No. Form inputs are processed in-memory only. If you opt in to email capture, we store your email + the report PDF — nothing else.

How accurate is the projection model?

For the next year (year n+1) when we have 5+ years of history, projections are usually within ±10 percentage points of actual. For year n+2 and beyond, uncertainty grows. We always show a confidence band, never a single number alone.

Why don't you list every school in Singapore yet?

v1 covers the 25 schools where we have the cleanest validated data, anchored on Central and East. We're expanding to all ~184 SG primary schools over the next few months. If a school you care about is missing, email {{ contact_email }}.

Will you cover Hong Kong K1 admissions next?

That's on the roadmap, yes. But not until SG P1 is rock-solid.

Can I see the source code or methodology?

Methodology is fully documented on the Methodology page. The engine is closed-source for now to avoid bad-faith copies; the methodology is open.

I run a school / I'm from MOE — there's an issue with my data.

Email {{ takedown_email }} with the specific URL and concern. We respond within 5 business days and correct verified data errors within 48 hours.
